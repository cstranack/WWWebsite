Frequently Asked Questions



How do I change my password?

If you've forgotten your password, when you go to log in, click on 'Log in now' and then on the 'Forgotten password?' link underneath the log in fields. This will allow a reset password link to be emailed to the email address associated with your account.

Alternatively if you are in the app and would just like to change your password, then click on 'Settings' in the bottom right hand corner and then 'Change Password'. This will take you through the steps to enter a new password.

How can I see my orders?

Wireless Waiter has three ways to view orders you have placed through our app. If you have selected a venue, then you can click on the 'Orders' icon at the bottom of the screen. 'Current Orders' will show you any open orders you have at the venue and 'Order History' will show you all closed orders you have with that venue.

If you would like to see all orders you've placed across any venue then click on 'Settings' and 'Order History', this will then show you all orders you've ever placed through Wireless Waiter, regardless of venue.

I don't know where to collect my order?

If your venue is offering a collection service then we will notify you when your order is ready to collect, and your collection point, with a map to guide you, will be shown at the bottom of the order summary screen. The collection code you will need in order to pick up your items will also be shown.

If you've already had to put in your seat or table number, then your order will be brought to you when it's ready.

How can I log out of Wireless Waiter?

Wireless Waiter automatically keeps you logged in to our system and will automatically take you to a venue if you still have orders open. If you would like to log out for any reason then please go to 'Settings' and click on the 'log out' link next to your name.

How do I change venue?

Wireless Waiter will automatically log you in to the app and load up a venue if you still have an open order with that venue. If you would like to change to a different venue then please click on the down arrow next to your current venue and location at the top of the screen. If you then click 'Change' next to your current venue name it will take you back to select a new venue.

How do I change my table or seat number?

If you have already selected a venue, this will be displayed at the top of your screen. Clicking on the down arrow next to your venue name will take you back to allow you to re-enter your table or seat number.

How do I change my location in the venue?

If you have already selected a venue, this will be displayed at the top of your screen. Clicking on the down arrow next to your venue and current location details will take you back to allow you to change your location within the venue.

How can I recommend a new venue for Wireless Waiter?

If you have a great venue in mind that would benefit from using the Wireless Waiter platform then please let us know by clicking on 'Settings' in the bottom right hand corner of the app and then 'Contact Us'. If you choose 'Suggest a Venue' as your reason, you can then recommend a venue to us and we will be in touch with them to get them set-up on our system, so you can enjoy using Wireless Waiter from another of your favourite venues.

How can I add my payment details to the app?

There are two methods for adding your payment details in the app. When you go to place an order and click on 'Checkout' any saved payment details will be displayed. If you haven't saved your details then you can add a new payment method by clicking on the link provided. You then also have the option to save this payment method for future use.

Alternatively, if you click on 'Settings' and 'Manage payment method' you will be taken through and be able to add or delete your payment details from the system. Your payment details will only have been saved with your agreement.

How can I delete my payment details from the app?

If you need to delete a payment method from our app then please click on 'Settings' and 'Manage payment method', this will show you any payment details you have saved on the system and give you the ability to delete a payment method by click on the 'x'.

Where are my payment details stored?

Any payment methods you add to Wireless Waiter are stored with our secure third party payment provider 'Stripe'. We as Wireless Waiter don't have access to your payment details.

When will I be charged for my order?

We take a 'payment intent' when you place your order, but your card will only be charged when your order is ready to collect.

How can I pre-order items before I arrive at a venue?

If your venue offers a pre-order service then you can pre-order food, drinks or merchandise to be ready for collection when you arrive at the venue. If you place your order in the normal way, when you click on 'All done?' to take you to our 'Checkout' screen then you can click on the 'ASAP' timeslot and it will open up all the pre-order slots available. Please choose the time you would like to collect your order and then 'Checkout' as normal. The venue will see when you have pre-ordered your items for and they will be ready for collection at that time. You will be notified as usual when your order is ready. You can also choose to pre-order items to be ready in the interval by choosing 'Interval'.

Why can't I pre-order items?

If your venue is offering a 'Table service', so bringing items directly to your table or seat then all orders will be prepared and delivered to you as soon as possible. If this is the case 'ASAP' will be displayed when you go in to the order summary screen to check out and you won't be able to click to bring up additional pre-order timeslots.

Why can I only amend some of my orders?

You can only amend an order up to the time your order is being prepared. Once you receive a notification that your order is being prepared then the staff at your venue are already getting things ready so you can no longer make changes. You can however easily just put in an additional order though if there's something that you've forgotten.

I received a notification that my order had been modified, how do I know what's changed?

By clicking on any notification you receive from the Wireless Waiter system it will take you in to the Order Summary page for that order. Any items that have had to be modified by your venue will appear crossed out on your order summary. Your order total will also have been amended to reflect the new amount that will be charged to your card. Sometimes a venue has to remove items if they have run out during service.

My order has been modified can I add something new instead?

If your order has been modified before your venue's staff have started preparing it then you will still have the chance to amend your order and add new items. From the order summary screen click on the 'Amend' link and it will take you back to amend your order. If the venue has already started preparing your order then you won't be able to amend it, however, if you need to you can always just place another order for the required items.

I'm not receiving notifications when my orders are ready?

You will have been asked to allow notifications when you first registered with Wireless Waiter, you can always change your original choice by going into settings on your device and accessing the settings for Wireless Waiter from there. Enable notifications to be able to see automated updates on how your orders are progressing.
