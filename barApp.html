Bar App

The Bar app is a tablet app to allow Bar Staff to view and fulfil orders:







A theatre has the option to route orders to different bars for fulfilment, any number of bars or outlets can be added to ease congestion




Bar staff have the ability to open or shut their bar or open for pre-orders only




Bar staff have the ability to switch off unavailable stock in real-time and amend customer orders




All historic orders can be viewed on the app for information

Covid Safety

  • Customers can automatically be routed to the nearest bar based on their location within the venue
  • All payments are taken remotely to reduce unnecessary contact
